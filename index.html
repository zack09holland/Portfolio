Azure Maps Demo

April 2020

Proof of concept web app built to show functionality and capabilities of Azure Map services for a client. I scoped out and designed all UX requirements to provide a simple way to showcase a variety of data layers, including choropleths, weather maps, heat maps, bubble layers, and more. Users can add a variety of file types, including shapefiles, XML, KML, and configure design options for the layers to meet their needs.

What2Do

September 2019

Have a trip coming up or want to know what's happening in your area during a certain time period? What2Do is there for you. This site allows users to find events and restaurants at your destination. It uses Ticketmaster, Yelp, and Zomato APIs to fetch data. Front end is built with handlebars and is deployed using heroku. Other tech used Node.js, Express.js.
